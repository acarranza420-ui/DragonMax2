# Living Background / Realm System

## Realms
- Dragon Realm: dragons, embers, mountain skies, castles
- Dark Wizard Realm: towers, magic books, runes, purple mist
- Vampire Queen Realm: gothic castles, red moons, candlelight, bats, rose petals
- Anime Warrior Realm: energy storms, floating islands, tournament arenas
- Martial Arts Realm: temples, dojos, waterfalls, cherry blossoms
- Fitness Arena: dragon gym, champion arena, torch-lit training ground
- Documentary Realm: maps, globe, space, ruins
- Music Realm: neon city, fantasy concert stage

## Animation Rules for Fire Stick
- Use subtle looping clips or image sequences only.
- Prefer 1080p compressed assets.
- Disable heavy particle effects by default.
- Cache thumbnails and backgrounds.
