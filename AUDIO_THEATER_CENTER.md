# Audio & Theater Center

Kodi does not expose a true 11.2 speaker layout selector. For 11.2 systems, your AVR/processor normally expands passthrough audio to your speaker layout.

## Recommended Profiles
- Movie Night
- Anime Theater
- Concert Hall
- Late Night
- Reference Audio

## Kodi Guidance
- Number of channels: usually 7.1 for multichannel PCM setups
- Allow passthrough: ON when your AVR supports it
- Dolby Digital / Dolby Digital Plus / TrueHD: ON if supported
- DTS / DTS-HD: ON if supported
- Use speaker test media to verify routing
