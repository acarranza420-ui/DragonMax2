# Office Panel

The Office Panel is a shortcut dashboard for work tools. Kodi is not a full office browser, so use this as a launcher/guide.

## Recommended Tiles
- Internet Browser: Amazon Silk Browser or another browser installed on Fire TV
- Google Docs: docs.google.com shortcut through browser
- Google Drive: drive.google.com shortcut through browser
- Email: Gmail, Outlook, or company webmail through browser/app
- CompanyCam: CompanyCam web shortcut or Android app if supported on your device
- Calendar: Google Calendar or Outlook web shortcut
- Work Files: SMB/NAS/Google Drive/OneDrive, depending on your setup

## Security
- Do not store passwords in Kodi settings files.
- Use account sign-in inside the official app/browser only.
- Keep work and personal profiles separate.
